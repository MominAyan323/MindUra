About MindUra

MindUra is a smart education platform designed to connect students, parents, and tuition classes in one powerful system. Our goal is to make tuition discovery simple, structured, and transparent for every learner.

We help students find the best tuition classes based on class, subject, and location while empowering tuition teachers with digital presence, student management, and growth opportunities. MindUra is building a future where every city has organized, system-driven education instead of unstructured local coaching.
